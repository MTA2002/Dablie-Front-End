Programs

Youth Club

More than just a club, we're a community dedicated to empowering young minds and hearts. Through engaging workshops, social events, and diverse learning modules, we equip you with the tools and knowledge to thrive in all aspects of life.

Ready to

Master essential life skills like anger management, financial literacy, public speaking, and more?

Develop your leadership potential through workshops on negotiation, team building, and conflict resolution?

Connect with like-minded peers through social events, sports activities, and cultural celebrations?

Earn Bronze, Silver, and Gold certifications as you progress through our learning modules?

Here's a glimpse of what awaits you

Educational Workshops

Dive deep into topics like anger management, financial literacy, first aid, and more. Gain valuable knowledge and practical skills for life.

Leadership Training

Discover your inner leader through workshops and activities that hone your communication, teamwork, and conflict resolution skills.

Social & Sports Clubs

Make friends, have fun, and explore your interests with our diverse range of social and sports clubs, from cooking and calligraphy to basketball and debating.

Certifications

Earn recognition for your achievements and commitment through our tiered program, unlocking exciting opportunities and experiences.

Trips and Events

Embark on unforgettable journeys and participate in engaging events that broaden your horizons and connect you with other young Muslims.

And that's not all! We offer a wide range of additional modules, covering topics like:

Dealing with family and friends

Setting and achieving goals

Public speaking and communication skills

Basic car maintenance and DIY skills

Responsible financial management

Understanding politics and current affairs
