About the Dablie
Foundation

At the Dablie Foundation, we believe in the transformative power of individuals. We ignite the spark of change by inspiring, empowering, and connecting people across Ethiopia and beyond. We cultivate responsible and effective leadership through education, economic empowerment, and system-thinking, fostering a generation of community builders equipped to tackle challenges and create a brighter future.

Our Story

We believe that investing in education, economic empowerment, and leadership development can empower communities to thrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, empower, and link individuals where they shape their future.
