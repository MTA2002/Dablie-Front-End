Membership

Membership is open to both individuals and businesses.

If you are an individual, you have two membership options

Member

ETB 1,200/year

  • Linkage with financial and non-financial institutions
  • Access to support programs hosted by EYEA including business competitions
  • Access to Educational workshops and trainings
  • Access to Networking events

Member Plus

ETB 2,400/year

  • Access to support programs and projects hosted by EYEA including business competitions
  • Access to training and mentorship programs
  • Access to Networking events
  • Promotion of business ideas
  • Exclusive chances to pitch business ideas or projects to potential investors
  • Access to educational workshops on entrepreneurship

Small

Medium
