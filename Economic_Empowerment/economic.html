The Dablie Foundation
Economic Empowerment Program

At the Dablie Foundation, we believe in empowering individuals and communities to achieve economic prosperity and self-reliance. That's why we offer a range of programs designed to support entrepreneurs and small business owners on their journey to success.

Approaches

Mentorship and Guidance: We connect aspiring and established entrepreneurs with experienced mentors who offer valuable advice, support, and networking opportunities.

Capacity Building Workshops: We provide practical workshops and training programs that equip individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the business world.

Funding Opportunities: We offer access to financial resources through microloans, grants, and partnerships, helping entrepreneurs launch and grow their businesses.

Networking Events: We create opportunities for businesses to connect, expand their networks, and access valuable resources.

Product & Service Showcases: We provide platforms for businesses to showcase their products and services, reaching new customers and building their brand awareness.

Workshops & Exhibitions: We offer informative workshops and engaging exhibitions that cover diverse business topics, fostering learning and collaboration.

Our aim

Create jobs: Stimulate economic growth and create employment opportunities within the community.

Contribute to local economies: Strengthen local businesses and foster a vibrant economic ecosystem.

Achieve financial independence: Empower individuals and families to build sustainable livelihoods.
