Membership

Membership is open to both individuals and businesses.

If you are an individual, you have one membership option

Standard Member

ETB 1,800/year

  • Connect with fellow members and experts at exclusive networking events, building valuable connections and friendships.
  • Access workshops on entrepreneurship, career development, and more.
  • Gain knowledge and expertise through entrepreneurship workshops, designed to help you thrive in your chosen field.
  • Get the support you need to turn your ideas into reality with Dablie-hosted programs, including business competitions and mentorship opportunities.
  • Access resources and partnerships with financial and non-financial institutions, opening doors to new possibilities.
  • Get regular updates on Foundation initiatives and events.
  • Volunteer your time and talents to make a difference.
  • Connect with like-minded individuals and build a strong support network.

Standard Member

ETB 24,000/year

  • Expand your network through exclusive networking events with like-minded businesses and industry leaders.
  • Gain valuable insights with access to all educational workshops on entrepreneurship, enhancing your team's knowledge and skills.
  • Participate in Dablie-hosted business competitions and support programs, gaining valuable feedback and potential funding opportunities.
  • Access business development support programs, tailored to guide your organization towards sustainable growth and positive community impact.
  • Receive information and assistance in accessing funding sources aligned with your business goals.
  • Engage and connect with industry experts for guidance and consultations, propelling your organization forward.
  • Regular updates on Dablie Foundation initiatives and events.
  • Opportunities to volunteer and contribute to meaningful projects.
  • A sense of belonging to a supportive and impactful community.

Premium Member
(all Standard benefits +)

  • Exclusive networking: Build strategic partnerships through networking events with large corporations and Dablie's extensive network.
  • Tailored support: Benefit from personalized guidance and assistance from Dablie experts to navigate challenges and achieve your goals.
  • Enhanced visibility: Gain representation and advocacy for your organization's interests, amplifying your voice within the community.
  • Employee development: Equip your team with training programs designed to enhance their skills and contribute to your organization's success.

Corporate Partner
(all Premium benefits +)

  • Executive-level engagement: Participate in exclusive advisory boards and leadership forums to shape the future of the Muslim community.
  • Brand recognition: Gain prominent sponsorship opportunities at Dablie events and initiatives, increasing your brand visibility.
  • Social impact: Make a significant impact by supporting Dablie's mission through strategic partnerships and collaborative projects.
  • Access tailored training programs and workshops designed specifically for your organization's needs.
  • Collaborate on strategic partnerships with the Dablie Foundation and its partners, driving impactful initiatives that benefit the community and your organization.
  • Receive personalized guidance and assistance from dedicated Dablie Foundation experts, tailored to your organization's unique needs and aspirations.
  • Enjoy priority registration and discounted rates for premium events like the Business Forum, gaining deeper insights and fostering stronger connections.
