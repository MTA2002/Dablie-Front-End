Events

We believe in creating vibrant spaces for connection, learning, and inspiration by hosting events that uplift, engage, and empower the community in Ethiopia. Join us for motivational workshops, insightful panels, and networking opportunities led by prominent speakers, community leaders, and industry experts. Connect with fellow changemakers, share ideas, and discover new pathways to a brighter future.

Youth Club Events

Engaging workshops and activities: Develop leadership skills, explore career paths, and connect with peers who share your aspirations.

Interactive discussions and debates: Tackle relevant issues, voice your opinions, and learn from diverse perspectives.

Fun and cultural celebrations: Celebrate your heritage, build friendships, and foster a sense of belonging.

Dablie Business Forum Events

Inspiring talks and workshops:  Learn from successful entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and investors.

Networking opportunities and business clinics:  Connect with potential partners, mentors, and collaborators.

Skill-building sessions and panel discussions: Gain practical knowledge to launch or grow your business, access funding, and navigate the market.

Whether you're seeking knowledge, inspiration, or connection, our events offer something for everyone. Stay tuned for upcoming events by visiting our website for the latest calendar and registration details, and follow us on social media for announcements and updates. Together, let's turn these events into catalysts for positive change. Join us and be part of something extraordinary!
