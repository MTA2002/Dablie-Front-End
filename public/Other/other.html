Programs

Other Programs

At the Dablie Foundation, we believe in fostering a sense of belonging and support for all members of our community. That's why we offer a variety of inclusive programs that cater to the diverse needs and interests of our members ,especially our elders, parents, and mothers.

Our aim

Build connections: Create a sense of belonging and foster intergenerational relationships.

Provide support: Offer resources and programs that cater to the specific needs of different groups.

Enhance knowledge: Expand your professional network and build valuable connections.

Promote wellbeing: Offer wellness activities and programs for individuals of all ages.

Programs

Elders Program

We create spaces for our elders to connect, share their experiences, participate in tailored activities like sports and wellness programs, and benefit from religious programs conducted in their own languages.

Ziyara Program

 Designed to honor our elders, this program facilitates visits and interactions aimed at fostering intergenerational connections and providing support

Language Training

For those who want to deepen their understanding of important texts, we offer language training to enhance their comprehension.

Workshops and Activities

We host a variety of workshops, activities, and events tailored to the interests of diverse members within our community.

We're always seeking to expand our inclusive programming. If you have ideas, please get in touch! Let's build a vibrant, supportive community where everyone feels valued and has opportunities to grow.
