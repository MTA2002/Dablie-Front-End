The Dablie Foundation
Business Forum

Imagine a thriving community of entrepreneurs and business leaders, united by a shared vision of economic empowerment. At the Dablie Business Forum, this vision becomes reality.

Our aim

Connect: Build lasting relationships with like-minded individuals, fostering
collaboration and mutual support.

Share knowledge: Learn from experienced mentors and industry experts through
insightful workshops and discussions.

Contribute to economic development: Be part of a collective effort to strengthen
the business community and create positive change.

Benefits

Working and investing together: Find partners, investors, and customers within our trusted network.

Know each other and buy from each other: Strengthen community bonds and support fellow businesses.

Networking events: Expand your professional network and build valuable connections.

Workshops and mentorship programs: Gain new skills, knowledge, and guidance from industry experts.

Access to resources: Benefit from exclusive resources and opportunities tailored to entrepreneurs.

Become part of a vibrant network of passionate leaders committed to building a brighter future for their communities. Apply to the Leadership Program that aligns with your aspirations and become the leader you were meant to be!
