Our Story

The Dablie Foundation is a newly established organization on a mission to uplift and empower our community. We envision a thriving community, self-reliant and prosperous, where individuals can reach their full potential. Our focus is on three key pillars: education, economic development, and leadership development. By investing in these areas, we aim to nurture the next generation of leaders who are committed to serving their communities and shaping a brighter future.

We recognize that the road to empowerment requires more than just good intentions. It demands strategic investment, and that's where the Dablie Foundation steps in. Instead of solely relying on charity, Dablie Foundation seeks to foster sustainable solutions that empower individuals and communities. We envision ourselves as a leading hub that connects funding and research & development (R&D) with the organizations working tirelessly to serve the Muslim community. We believe that by breaking down barriers to funding and fostering innovation, we can unlock the true potential of countless initiatives.

Our Pillars

Education

Investing in education is the cornerstone of our mission. We support access to quality education for all, nurturing future leaders and unlocking potential.

Economic Development

We empower individuals and communities through economic opportunities. We support entrepreneurship, small businesses, and initiatives that drive financial stability and self-reliance.

Leaders Development

We cultivate strong, ethical leaders who embody Islamic principles and serve their communities with dedication and vision.

Building a Network of Support
We envision a platform where collaboration and partnership reign supreme. By bringing together a network of passionate supporters, investors, and community-based organizations, we can identify and fund impactful initiatives aligned with our values. . Together, we will identify and invest in opportunities that align with our shared values, fostering long-term relationships that drive sustainable change.
Education and Beyond
Our efforts encompass various aspects of community life. We invest in education, ensuring access to quality learning opportunities for all. We promote economic development projects to foster well-being and prosperity. Furthermore, we cultivate leadership qualities in the next generation, empowering them to serve their communities with integrity and vision. By investing in these crucial areas, we aim to build a stronger, more resilient community that thrives on its own merits.
Research & Development
We envision a platform where collaboration and partnership reign supreme. By bringing together a network of passionate supporters, investors, and community-based organizations, we can identify and fund impactful initiatives aligned with our values. Together, we will identify and invest in opportunities that align with our shared values, fostering long-term relationships that drive sustainable change.
Leading the Way
The Dablie Foundation aspires to become the leading institution dedicated to providing sustained support to the Muslim community in Ethiopia. We envision ourselves as a one-stop hub that offers uninterrupted and sustainable support, preserving our cultural and moral values while propelling the community towards a brighter future.

Join us on the journey

Building a lasting impact takes time, dedication, and collective effort. We invite you to join us on this journey. Together, we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of individuals, families, and the entire Muslim community in Ethiopia. Let's create a future where prosperity, self-reliance, and empowerment are not just aspirations, but lived realities.

Become part of the change. Become part of the Dablie Foundation.
